Teams often lose time on large text exports because the first tool treats them like small notes.

The useful test is simple: decide whether the file needs quick reading, search, light edits, or full editing before choosing the tool.

Before changing tools:
- Start by opening a copy of the file, not the only original.
- Confirm the encoding if the text appears broken or unreadable.
- Use a plain-text reader or editor instead of a word processor.
